Caring for Your McCluskey Pottery

Your pottery is made to be used and loved every day. With proper care, it will last for generations.

Dishwasher Safe

All our pottery is dishwasher safe. Use a gentle cycle with mild detergent.

Microwave Safe

Perfect for reheating your tea or warming soup. Safe for everyday use.

Daily Care

Washing

  • While dishwasher safe, hand washing will keep your pottery looking its best longer
  • Use warm water and mild dish soap
  • A soft sponge or cloth is ideal - avoid abrasive cleaners
  • For stubborn stains, soak in warm soapy water before washing

Storage

  • Stack plates with felt pads or paper towels between them
  • Store mugs upright to protect handles
  • Keep in a dry cupboard away from extreme temperature changes

Special Care Tips

For Tea & Coffee Stains

Mix baking soda with a little water to form a paste. Gently rub on stains, let sit for 10 minutes, then wash as normal. This natural method preserves the glaze while removing stains.

For Cutlery Marks

These grey marks from cutlery are actually metal deposits on the surface. They can be removed with a paste of baking soda or a specialised ceramic cleaner.

What to Avoid

  • Sudden temperature changes: Don't move pottery directly from freezer to oven
  • Harsh chemicals: Bleach and strong cleaners can damage the glaze
  • Metal scourers: These can scratch the surface
  • Dropping or knocking: While durable, pottery can chip or break from impacts

The Beauty of Use

Remember, your McCluskey pottery is meant to be used. Over time, it may develop a patina - tiny surface crazing or slight colour changes that add character. This is not a flaw but rather the pottery developing its own unique story through daily use.

Like a favourite pair of jeans or a well-loved book, your pottery becomes more beautiful with age and use. The memories made around these pieces - the morning coffees, the family dinners, the quiet moments - become part of their story.
