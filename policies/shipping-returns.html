Shipping & Returns

Free shipping on all orders over €75 within Ireland!
Each piece is carefully wrapped with love and shipped with care.

Shipping Information

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. During busy periods (especially around holidays), processing may take up to 5 business days. Each piece is carefully inspected before shipping to ensure it meets our quality standards.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

  • Ireland: €5 flat rate (2-3 business days) - FREE over €75
  • UK: €12 (3-5 business days)
  • Europe: €18 (5-7 business days)
  • USA/Canada: €25 (7-10 business days)
  • Rest of World: €30 (10-14 business days)

Packaging

Your pottery is precious to us. Each piece is:

  • Individually wrapped in tissue paper
  • Secured with biodegradable packing materials
  • Placed in a sturdy box with extra cushioning
  • Sealed with our signature McCluskey Pottery sticker

Returns & Exchanges

Our Promise

We want you to absolutely love your McCluskey Pottery. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging should be retained if possible
  • Custom or personalised orders cannot be returned

How to Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us at hello@mccluskeypottery.ie with your order number
  2. We'll provide a return authorization and address
  3. Pack the item securely (we recommend reusing the original packaging)
  4. Ship the item back to us
  5. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund

Damages and Issues

Despite our best efforts, sometimes things happen during shipping. If your order arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • We'll arrange a replacement or full refund immediately
